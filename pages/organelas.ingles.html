Plant Cell Organelles

Explore the main organelles of the plant cell and their functions.
Also check out the representations in the model!

Chloroplast

carries out the photosynthesis process in plant cells and protists (algae)

Ribosomes

Produce proteins, which can be free in the cytoplasm or on the rough endoplasmic reticulum.

Rough Endoplasmic Reticulum

has ribosomes attached to the surface of its
membranes. Protein synthesis for export outside the cell

Smooth Endoplasmic Reticulum

responsible for lipid synthesis, detoxification of
substances, storage of calcium ions,
and production of some plant hormones.

Golgi Complex

secretes, modifies, and sends secretions outside the cell (Cellular Secretion)

Peroxisomes

break down amino acids and lipids, helping in cellular detoxification

Plant Vacuole

stores water, salts, and nutrients, maintaining the turgor pressure that supports the plant

Mitochondria

produces energy (ATP) through cellular respiration.

Nucleus

controls cell activities and the hereditary traits of organisms.
